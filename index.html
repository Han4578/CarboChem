Welcome to CarboChem!

Here, you can build any carbon based chemical structures your want!

Blue lines indicate where you can add an element, click on them to add one.
To change which element to add, click on the second button on the right to open the menu.
To change which bond to add, click on the third button on the right to open the menu.

Reset button resets the game.
Auto button adds a hydrogen element onto every blue line.

Del button toggles delete mode.
In delete mode, the outermost elements can the last carbon in the carbon chain is marked as red. Click on them to delete it.

Shortcut keys:
C - Carbon
H - Hydrogen
O - Oxygen
L - Chlorine
B - Bromine
I - Iodine
R - Reset
A - Auto
D - Delete
1 - Single Bond
2 - Double Bond
3 - Triple Bond
